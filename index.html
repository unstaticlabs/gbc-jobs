The Bigger Vision

Hey! I'm SBFH. I'm an engineer, a kinky sub boy, and a content creator. My main driver is freedom, and I'm building toward the next version of the internet: a free web for communities. My beliefs and values are well summarized in the "Fuck The VCs" manifesto.

This journey will not be easy. We're going up against much bigger players. But we cannot win if we do not play. At least, we can have fun trying.

It starts with claiming our humanity: building community and owning back our identities.

I want to build a space online where we can be ourselves, and where we can be free.

Layer 1

Community

Good Boys Club is home. This layer is not mainly about tech. It is about people, culture, safety, and belonging. We are building a positive, affirming space for submissive men.

Layer 2

Social Infrastructure

Smash is a 1:N messaging and curated content-sharing protocol. Users own their identities and data. With interoperable identity, they can join multiple neighborhoods. Like in the real world, neighborhoods are where people meet and communities form.

Layer 3

Human Connection

Social networks became less and less social. KinkVerse aims to bring back human connection: a neighborhood built for kinksters, by kinksters. Long term, the KinkVerse's neighborhood governance should belong to the community. Enough of opportunistic capitalists taking over our communities and culture.

Layer 4

Content Creation

Content creation is now a major part of the web. We are building tools that help creators become more resilient and own their content. A nice side effect: it also makes my life as a creator much easier.

We've got the vision, now let's have some fun.
